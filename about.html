About Scout Browser

Our Mission

Scout Browser was created to give parents more control and peace of mind while enabling their children to access YouTube's educational content. We recognized that traditional web browsers and YouTube's built-in safety features don't provide parents with sufficient tools to curate content according to their family's specific values and educational goals.

Our dedicated browser application uses advanced AI technology to assist parents in analyzing video content—not just titles and thumbnails, but available transcripts and visual elements when enabled. This approach helps parents create a viewing environment that aligns with their family values and supports healthy digital consumption habits.

I've seen too many young kids spend hours on overstimulating YouTube content—wasting the curiosity and intelligence I know they have. It reminded me how much media shapes young minds. Growing up in Silicon Valley, I had access to content that pushed me to build things and taught me math, engineering, and problem-solving skills—lessons that shaped my career. I want more kids to get that kind of high-quality, educational exposure.

Scout Browser enables that. Parents can use tools to help filter inappropriate or concerning content while highlighting educational material that supports their family's learning goals and reflects their values.
