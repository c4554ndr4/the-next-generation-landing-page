KidBrowser Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

KidBrowser collects and processes the following information:

  • YouTube video content (titles, descriptions, transcripts, thumbnails) for analysis
  • Your application settings and content filtering preferences
  • Basic usage analytics to improve the application

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Analyzing YouTube content for appropriateness using AI
  • Filtering search results and recommendations in real-time
  • Storing your family's content preferences locally
  • Improving KidBrowser's filtering accuracy and functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is:

  • Processed locally on your device whenever possible
  • Transmitted securely using HTTPS when cloud analysis is required
  • Not stored permanently on our servers
  • Not shared with any third parties except for AI content analysis services

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services for content analysis:

  • AI content analysis services for video safety evaluation
  • These services only receive content data for analysis, not personal information

5. Children's Privacy

KidBrowser is designed specifically for children's safety:

  • We do not collect personal information from children
  • Content filtering happens in real-time without storing viewed content
  • Parents maintain full control over all settings and preferences

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Control all content filtering preferences
  • Access your stored settings at any time
  • Email us at cassandra at melax dot com for support or questions
  • Uninstall KidBrowser and remove all local data at any time

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the application accordingly.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at cassandra at melax dot com.

This privacy policy is effective as of December 2024
