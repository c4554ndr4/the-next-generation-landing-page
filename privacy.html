Content Monitor Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The Content Monitor extension collects and processes the following information:

  • Content from web pages you visit (text, images, videos)
  • Your extension settings and preferences
  • Parent key for authentication

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Analyzing web content for inappropriate material
  • Authenticating your access to the extension
  • Storing your preferences
  • Improving the extension's functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is:

  • Transmitted securely to our servers using HTTPS
  • Not shared with any third parties except as required for content analysis

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase for storing user data
  • OpenAI, Anthropic, and Gemini for content analysis
  • Render.com for hosting the backend

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored preferences
  • Email us at cassandra at melax dot com to delete your data
  • Opt-out of content monitoring at any time

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the extension accordingly.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at cassandra at melax dot com.

This privacy policy is effective as of April 15, 2025
