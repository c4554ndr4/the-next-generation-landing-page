Scout Browser Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Important Notice

Scout Browser is an independent, third-party application and is not affiliated with, endorsed, or sponsored by YouTube or Google. It is designed as a tool for parents to manage their children's YouTube viewing experience. No filtering technology is 100% accurate, and parental supervision remains essential.

1. Information We Collect

Scout Browser is designed with privacy in mind and collects minimal information necessary to provide the service:

  • Content Analysis Data: YouTube video metadata (titles, descriptions, available transcripts, thumbnails) for AI analysis when content filtering is enabled
  • User Preferences: Your application settings and content filtering preferences, stored locally on your device
  • Basic Usage Analytics: Anonymous technical information to improve application performance and stability
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information from users, including children

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Analyzing YouTube content appropriateness using AI when enabled by parental settings
  • Providing content filtering and recommendation controls based on your configured preferences
  • Storing your family's content preferences locally on your device
  • Improving Scout Browser's functionality and AI analysis capabilities
  • Technical support and troubleshooting when requested

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy and security are our priorities:

  • Local Storage: All user preferences and settings are stored locally on your device
  • Temporary Processing: Content analysis data is processed securely and temporarily when AI analysis is requested
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store video content, viewing history, or personal information on our servers
  • Secure Transmission: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • No Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

4. Third-Party Services

Scout Browser uses the following third-party services:

  • AI Content Analysis Services: For video safety evaluation when enabled by parents
  • YouTube API: To access video metadata and content (subject to YouTube's Terms of Service)
  • These services receive only the minimum data necessary for content analysis and do not receive personal information

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Scout Browser is designed as a parental tool with strong privacy protections for children:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Parental Control: All settings and preferences are controlled by parents/guardians
  • No Profiles or Accounts: The application does not require children to create accounts or profiles
  • Local Processing: Content filtering happens locally whenever possible
  • No Behavioral Tracking: We do not track or profile children's viewing behavior

6. Limitations and Disclaimers

Important Limitations

  • Filtering Accuracy: AI content analysis is not 100% accurate and may produce false positives or miss some content
  • Content Availability: Analysis effectiveness depends on availability of video transcripts, metadata, and other factors
  • Parental Responsibility: Parents remain responsible for supervising their children's online activities
  • Regular Review Required: Settings and preferences should be reviewed and adjusted regularly
  • Technical Limitations: Content filtering may not work with all video types or formats

7. Your Rights and Controls

As a parent using Scout Browser, you have:

  • Full Control: Complete control over all content filtering preferences and settings
  • Data Access: Ability to access and modify your stored settings at any time
  • Data Deletion: Ability to uninstall Scout Browser and remove all local data
  • Opt-out: Ability to disable any feature or stop using the service at any time
  • Support Access: Right to contact us for technical support or questions

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will post the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date
  • Significant changes may be communicated through the application
  • Continued use of Scout Browser after updates constitutes acceptance of the new policy

9. Legal Disclaimer

Scout Browser is provided "as is" without warranties of any kind. While we strive to provide effective content filtering tools, parents acknowledge that:

  • No automated filtering system is perfect
  • Parents remain solely responsible for their children's online safety
  • Regular supervision and communication with children about online content remains essential
