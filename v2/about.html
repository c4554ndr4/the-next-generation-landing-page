About The Next Generation Project

Our Mission

The Next Generation Project was created to help families align their digital experiences with their core values. Today's algorithms are designed to maximize engagement, not well-being, often leading children down harmful paths to polarizing ideologies, addictive content loops, and messaging that undermines the values parents are trying to instill.

We're building tools that give parents visibility and control without invading their children's privacy or creating adversarial relationships.

Team

Cassandra

Founder, The Next Generation Project
LinkedIn Profile

I've seen too many young kids spend hours on overstimulating YouTube compilations—wasting the curiosity and intelligence I know they have. It reminded me how much media shapes young minds. Growing up in Silicon Valley, I had access to content that pushed me to build things and taught me math, engineering, and social‑productivity skills—lessons that shaped my career. I want more kids to get that kind of high‑agency, builder‑mindset exposure.

This product does that. Parents can block 'TikTok slop,' inappropriate or frightening rabbit‑hole content, and influencers pushing harmful ideologies (mental-health self-diagnosis, toxic masculinity, nihilism) while surfacing material that inspires kids to build things, exercise agency, and stay connected with their parents.
